What is IELTS?

IELTS stands for International English Language Testing System. It is an internationally recognised English language proficiency test for non-native speakers who want to study or work in countries where English is the primary language of communication. The test assesses the four language skills: Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking.

There are two types of IELTS tests: Academic and General Training. The Academic test is for those who want to study at a university or college abroad.
